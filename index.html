CodePurpose

Helping Those Who Help Others Leverage Technology

A subsidiary of University of Utah's ACM Chapter

Mission

CodePurpose is devoted to helping non-profits and student organizations leverage technology to aid them in their goals. We are students who believe we can influence change in our community and better our technical skills by creating real world solutions to problems your organization may face. We also plan on hosting training workshops to promote techinical ability amongst underrepresented communities.

Want To Get Involved?

We are always looking for more support. If you want to take what you learn in the classroom and have an impact on the communities around you, we need you! You should also be self-motivated and willing to work with a team. Experience in impacting the world is our end goal. So, if you are willing to learn and contribute you will be a perfect fit.
